Picture Description: Flower Garden

This is a picture of a beautiful flower garden. People often visit gardens to see colorful flowers, take photos, and enjoy fresh air. Gardens are relaxing places to connect with nature.

Describing Places in English

When describing a place in English, you can mention:

  • What can you see there?
  • What activities can people do?
  • How does the place make you feel?
Example: Visitors enjoy walking among the colorful flowers in the garden.
