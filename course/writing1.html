Writing Topic 1

Menulis perkenalan diri

Materi

Ketika memperkenalkan diri dalam bahasa Inggris, kamu bisa menyebutkan nama, usia, asal, hobi, dan cita-cita. Contoh kalimat:
Hello, my name is Siti. I am twelve years old. I come from Jakarta. My hobby is drawing, and I want to be an architect.

Quiz

1. Which sentence is correct?


2. Which one is a hobby?


3. Which is a dream job?


4. Which city is mentioned in the example?


5. How old is Siti?
