Writing Topic 2

Menulis kegiatan sehari-hari

Materi

Dalam bahasa Inggris, kamu bisa menceritakan rutinitas sehari-hari. Contoh kalimat:
I wake up at six o'clock. I go to school at seven. After school, I play with my friends and do my homework in the evening.

Quiz

1. What time does the writer wake up?


2. When does the writer go to school?


3. What does the writer do after school?


4. When does the writer do homework?


5. Who does the writer play with?
