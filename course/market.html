Picture Description: Market

This is a picture of a traditional market. People come here to buy and sell various products like vegetables, fruits, fish, and meat. The market is usually crowded in the morning when everyone is shopping for their daily needs.

Descriptive Sentences in English

When you describe a picture, you usually start with what you see and then give more details. In English, descriptive sentences often answer questions like:

  • What is in the picture?
  • What are people doing?
  • Where is it happening?
Example: People are buying fresh vegetables in the traditional market.
