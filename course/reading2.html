Reading Topic 2

Latihan membaca tentang hobi

Text

My name is Rina. I have a hobby. My hobby is cooking. I like trying new recipes, especially cakes and cookies. Every weekend, I bake with my mother. We also share the food with our neighbors. Cooking makes me happy and creative.

Quiz

1. What is Rina's hobby?


2. What does Rina like to make?


3. When does Rina bake with her mother?


4. Who else enjoys Rina's cooking?


5. How does cooking make Rina feel?
