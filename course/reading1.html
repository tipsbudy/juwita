Reading Topic 1

Latihan membaca teks sederhana

Text

Anna is a student. She goes to school every day by bicycle. She likes to study English and Mathematics. After school, she often helps her mother at home. Anna also likes reading story books. She wants to be a teacher in the future.

Quiz

1. How does Anna go to school?


2. What subject does Anna like?


3. What does Anna do after school?


4. What is Anna's hobby?


5. What does Anna want to be in the future?
